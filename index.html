Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 805StreetFoods ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the 805StreetFoods mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account & Profile Information

When you create or use an account with the Services, we may collect:

  • Authentication details: Email address and a unique user identifier provided via Firebase Authentication when you sign in with:
    • Sign in with Apple
    • Sign in with Google
    • Email and password (admin-created accounts only â€” see Account Access Policy below)
  • Profile data: First and last name (if provided), display name, role (for example: user, vendor, venue, admin), and basic account preferences.
  • Vendor/venue links: For users with vendor or venue access, references to the truck(s) or venue(s) you manage.

Account Access Policy

805StreetFoods uses OAuth-only authentication to protect your account security. This means:

  • Primary sign-in methods: Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google
  • No self-service password accounts: Users cannot create email/password accounts themselves
  • Admin-created accounts only: If you prefer not to use Apple or Google sign-in, contact us at admin@805streetfoods.com and we can manually create an email/password account for you
  • No password storage or reset: We do not store passwords or offer password reset functionality for OAuth accounts. This eliminates common attack vectors like password reset exploits and credential stuffing
  • Enhanced security: OAuth providers (Apple, Google) handle authentication with industry-leading security practices, two-factor authentication, and breach monitoring

1.2 Usage & Analytics Data

We use Firebase and related tools to understand how the Services are used and to improve stability and performance. This may include:

  • Analytics events: Screens viewed, buttons tapped, features used, and basic engagement data (for example, favorite toggles, check-ins, ratings submitted).
  • Performance data: App startup times, network request performance, and other technical metrics.
  • Crash reports: Error details and stack traces when the app crashes or encounters critical errors.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, app version, and language/region settings.

1.3 User-Generated Content & Activity

Depending on how you use the Services, we may process:

  • Favorites: Food trucks and venues you mark as favorites or follow.
  • Check-ins: Records that you checked in at a particular event, along with metadata like the truck, venue, and time.
  • Badges & achievements: Which badges you have unlocked and related stats (such as visit counts, streaks, and unique trucks visited).
  • Ratings and notes: Star ratings and, if enabled, written notes or reviews you submit about trucks or venues. These may go through a moderation process.
  • Vendor & venue content: If you are a vendor or venue, information about your truck or venue, including schedules, events, links, dietary tags, and descriptive text.
  • Images: For vendors and admins, truck logos, banners, and food or venue photos that you upload, which are stored in Firebase Storage.

1.4 Location & Permissions

The Services may request access to certain device permissions. You can control these in your device settings at any time.

Location (Optional, Check-Ins Only)

When you attempt to check in to an event, we may request access to your device's location to verify that you are within 2 miles of the event.

  • We do not store your GPS coordinates. Location is used only momentarily on your device to verify distance.
  • Our systems store a boolean result (for example, locationVerified = true/false) and related check-in metadata, not your precise location history.
  • You can use the app without granting location access, but certain check-in and badge features may not work as intended.

Calendar (Optional)

If you choose to add an event to your device's calendar, the app will request permission to write events. We:

  • Only create events that you explicitly choose to add.
  • Do not read your existing calendar events.

Photos (Vendors & Admins Only)

If you upload logos, banners, or food photos, we request access to your photo library. The app only accesses and uploads the images you select.

Push Notifications (Optional)

With your permission, we use push notifications to alert you about:

  • Events for trucks or venues you've favorited
  • Event status changes (for example, cancellations or early closures)
  • Badge unlocks, reminders, and app updates

You can manage notification preferences both at the system level (iOS Settings) and, where available, inside the app.

1.5 Subscription & Entitlement Data (Vendors)

For vendors and venues using subscription features, we may store:

  • Subscription tier: For example, none/free, Basic, or Pro.
  • Subscription source: For example, web-based purchase or complimentary access.
  • Expiration information: Dates used to determine when access should be renewed or expire.
  • Featured placement: Flags that indicate whether a truck has purchased boosted visibility and until when.

Payment processing does not happen inside the app. Vendor payments are handled on our external website and by third-party payment processors. We do not collect or store credit card information in the mobile app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing and maintaining the Services: Authenticating you, loading your favorites, check-ins, badges, and event data.
  • Operating core features: Showing truck and venue information, event schedules, maps, and personalized recommendations.
  • Notifications: Sending you alerts you've requested or enabled, such as reminders for favorited trucks or status changes.
  • Vendor tools: Allowing vendors and venues to manage schedules, profiles, photos, and analytics for their trucks and locations.
  • Analytics and improvement: Understanding which features are used, fixing bugs, and improving performance and reliability.
  • Security and abuse prevention: Detecting suspicious activity, misuse of check-ins, or attempts to circumvent our systems.
  • Legal and compliance: Meeting our legal obligations and responding to lawful requests from authorities when required.

3. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Where data protection laws (such as the GDPR in the European Union or UK) apply, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the Services you request (for example, creating an account, saving favorites and check-ins).
  • Legitimate interests: For analytics, service improvement, and security, provided these are not overridden by your rights and interests.
  • Consent: For optional features like push notifications, location access, and calendar access. You can withdraw consent at any time via settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring, and Cloud Messaging.
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple, Apple Push Notification service (APNs), MapKit, WeatherKit, and other platform services.
  • Google Sign-In: Additional authentication option.
  • Square (Vendors Only): Vendors with Pro subscriptions may connect their Square accounts via OAuth to enable online ordering. We store OAuth tokens to access the vendor's Square catalog and facilitate order processing. Square's own privacy policy governs their handling of payment and transaction data. We do not access or store customer payment information.
  • Payment processors (via vendor website): When you subscribe as a vendor on our external website, payments are handled by third-party processors under their own terms and privacy policies.

These providers process your data on our behalf or as separate controllers, and each has its own privacy policy.

5. Data Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information:

  • With service providers that help us operate the Services (for example, Firebase and payment processors).
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • When necessary to protect our rights, property, or the safety of users or the public.
  • With your consent or at your direction (for example, when you choose to navigate to an external site or share a link).

Our primary infrastructure currently operates in data centers that may be located outside your home country. By using the Services, you understand that your data may be processed in these locations, subject to appropriate protections and agreements between us and our service providers.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide and improve the Services, or as required by law. In general:

  • Account data: Kept while your account is active. If your account becomes inactive or you request deletion, we will take reasonable steps to remove or anonymize your data, subject to legal and operational needs.
  • Vendor content: Truck and venue data is retained as long as it is actively used or managed in the system, or until removed by a vendor or admin.
  • Check-ins, badges, and activity: Retained to power your history, stats, and achievements unless and until you request deletion where feasible.
  • Analytics and logs: Retained according to Firebase and platform defaults (for example, analytics and crash reports are generally kept in aggregate form for a limited period).

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete certain personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements.
  • Restriction or objection: Request that we limit certain processing or object to certain uses, such as analytics, where applicable.
  • Portability (where applicable): Ask for a copy of certain data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights or to make a privacy-related request, please contact us at admin@805streetfoods.com. We may need to verify your identity before completing your request.

You also have the following controls:

  • Manage permissions (location, notifications, calendar, photos) in your device settings.
  • Update certain profile information directly in the app, where available.
  • Disable push notifications at any time via your device's settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and security features provided by our service providers (such as Firebase). However, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Widgets, Deep Links & Website

Widgets

If you use the 805StreetFoods home screen widget, data is shared between the app and widget using iOS App Groups, which store data locally on your device. No additional data is sent to our servers solely because you use the widget.

Universal Links & Deep Links

When you open links such as https://805streetfoods.com/... or streetfood://..., your device may open the app and navigate to a specific truck, venue, or event. These links primarily route you to content and do not themselves add new personal data to your profile beyond normal app usage.

External Websites

The Services may link to external websites (for example, vendor subscription management or truck menus). Your activity on those websites is governed by their own terms and privacy policies, not this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and may provide additional notice within the app or by email.

Your continued use of the Services after any changes take effect signifies your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
