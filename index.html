Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

This privacy policy describes how the 805StreetFoods mobile application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Authentication: When you sign in using Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In, we collect your email address and user identifier through Firebase Authentication
  • Profile Data: Display name, role (user/vendor/admin), and account preferences

Usage Data

  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect information about how you use the App, including screens viewed, actions taken, and feature usage
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects crash logs and error reports to help us improve app stability
  • Performance Data: Firebase Performance monitors app performance metrics such as startup time and network latency

User-Generated Content

  • Favorites: Food trucks you mark as favorites
  • Ratings & Reviews: Ratings you submit for food trucks
  • Vendor Content: If you're a vendor, we store truck profiles, schedules, and events you create
  • Images: Vendors and admins can upload logos, banners, and food photos, which are stored in Firebase Storage
  • Feedback: Bug reports, feature requests, and other feedback you submit

Device & Notification Data

  • Push Notification Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens and Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) device tokens are collected to send you notifications about your favorited food trucks
  • Notification Preferences: Your notification settings and per-truck notification preferences

Permissions We Request

Location (Optional)

When In Use Only. When you grant location permission, your location is used locally on your device to calculate distances to food trucks and venues. Your location data is never sent to our servers or stored remotely. All distance calculations happen on your device.

Calendar (Optional)

Used only when you explicitly choose to add an event to your device's calendar via the "Add to Calendar" button. We never read your existing calendar events—we only write events you choose to add.

Photo Library (Vendors & Admins Only)

If you're a vendor or admin, you can select photos from your library to upload as truck logos, venue banners, or food photos. The App only accesses photos you explicitly select.

Push Notifications (Optional)

We request permission to send you notifications about food truck events for trucks you've favorited. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings or within the App.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication & Accounts: To manage your account and authenticate you when you sign in
  • Core Features: To display food truck schedules, venues, events, and enable favorites
  • Notifications: To send you reminders about food truck events for your favorited trucks
  • Vendor Features: To enable vendors to create and manage their truck profiles and schedules
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve performance
  • Support: To respond to your feedback, bug reports, and support requests

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring, and Cloud Messaging
  • Apple Sign-In: For user authentication
  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication
  • WeatherKit (Apple): To display weather forecasts for venue locations. Weather data is fetched on-demand and cached locally on your device for 90 minutes. It is not stored on our servers.
  • Device Check (Apple): Firebase App Check uses Apple's Device Check framework to verify requests are coming from authentic devices, without collecting personal information

Each service operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes. We share data only:

  • With the third-party services listed above to operate the App
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

We do not engage in cross-app tracking or use your data for targeted advertising.

Your Controls

  • Permissions: Adjust location, calendar, photo library, and notification permissions in iOS Settings
  • Notification Preferences: Manage notification settings for individual trucks within the App
  • Favorites: Add or remove favorites at any time
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account within the App's settings. This will remove your account data, but publicly visible content you created (like ratings) may be retained for moderation purposes

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Vendor Content: Truck and venue data remains available until removed by the vendor or admin
  • Analytics & Crash Logs: Retained according to Firebase's standard policies (typically 60 days for raw data, longer for aggregate reports)
  • Deleted Accounts: Account data is removed within 30 days of deletion

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase services use encryption at rest
  • Access to backend systems is restricted and monitored
  • We use Firebase App Check to prevent unauthorized API access

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Widget Data Sharing

The App includes a home screen widget that displays upcoming food truck events. Data is shared between the App and widget using iOS App Groups (a secure, on-device storage mechanism). No data is transmitted off your device for widget functionality.

Universal Links & Deep Linking

The App supports Universal Links (https://805streetfoods.com URLs) and URL schemes (streetfood://) to enable deep linking. When you open these links, your device may navigate directly to specific content within the App. No personal information is transmitted through these links.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, if appropriate, posting a notice in the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, questions, or to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at admin@805streetfoods.com.
