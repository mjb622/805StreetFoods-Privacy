Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2nd, 2025

805StreetFoods (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (“the App”) handles information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you sign in using email, Google, or Apple, we store your account identifier through Firebase Authentication.
  • Vendor Information: Vendors may add or update information about their food trucks (e.g., name, description, schedule, and social media links).

We do not collect personal information beyond what is required for account creation and management.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and vendors.
  • To display truck schedules and related details within the app.
  • To allow vendors to manage their truck profiles and schedules.

We do not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties for advertising or analytics.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time within the app. Deletion removes your Firebase account and associated vendor data from our database. This action cannot be undone.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following services, which may handle data under their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) – Authentication, database
  • Apple – Sign in with Apple
  • Google – Google Sign-In

We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We rely on Firebase and Apple’s infrastructure to protect your data using industry-standard encryption.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last Updated” date.
